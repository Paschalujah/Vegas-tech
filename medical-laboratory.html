  • Renal function test
  • Liver function test

- Serology

Serology tests are helpful in the diagnosis of certain bacteria, parasitic and viral diseases. Tests under this include;

  • Retroviral screening(HIV infection)
  • Veneral disease research laboratory(VDRL) test for syphilis
  • Chlamydia detection
  • Hepatitis A, B and C screening
  • Serum/blood Tuberculosis
  • erpes Simplex Virus I & II
  • Serum H. Pylori test, etc.

- Haematology

We have a fully automated 6-part haematology analyser, giving you the advantage of a reticulocyte count in all full blood count(FBC) analysis. Some other tests we carry out include;

  • Clotting profile
  • Erythrocyte sedimentation rate
  • Blood group and genotype
  • Peripheral blood film analysis
  • Glucose 6-phosphate dehydrogenase(G6PD), etc.

- Medical Microbiology

We offer a deviation from conventional medical microbiology to a modern, automated medical microbiology practice. We boast of a biologically safe and aseptic analysis of microbiology samples.

  • State-of-the-art microbiology machine for identifying bacteria and fungi, including antibiotic and antifungal sensitivity.
  • Semen analysis; checks acrosome vitality, DNA fragmentation, motility and morphology of sperm cells.
  • Fully automated blood culture system that can detect organism growth from three(3) days of incubation.
